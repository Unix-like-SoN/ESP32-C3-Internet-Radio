## üí° Photography Tips

### Lighting
- ‚òÄÔ∏è **Best:** Daylight near window (avoid direct sun)
- üí° **Good:** LED lamp with diffuser
- ‚ùå **Avoid:** Yellow incandescent, harsh shadows

### Background
- ‚úÖ **Best:** White or light gray solid color
- ‚úÖ **Good:** Black for contrast
- ‚ùå **Avoid:** Cluttered, patterned, reflective surfaces

### Camera Settings
- **ISO:** 100-400 (low for clean images)
- **Focus:** Auto-focus or manual on subject
- **Exposure:** Slight overexposure OK (can darken in edit)

### Composition
- **Rule of thirds:** Place subject off-center
- **Angle:** 45¬∞ for depth, straight for technical
- **Margin:** Leave small space around edges
- **Focus:** Main subject sharp, background can be blurred
